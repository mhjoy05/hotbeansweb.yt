Job Specification

You will be responsible for safeguarding our organization's computer systems and networks against cyber threats such as hacking, malware, and data breaches. Key responsibilities include conducting regular risk and vulnerability assessments, designing and implementing robust security architectures, and leading incident response activities to manage and mitigate security breaches. The role also involves ensuring compliance with relevant laws and standards, developing and enforcing security policies, and providing security training to employees.

You will be responsible for developing and maintaining websites and web applications to ensure seamless user experiences. Key responsibilities include writing clean, efficient code, troubleshooting and debugging applications, and collaborating with designers and other developers to implement new features and functionalities. The role also involves staying updated with the latest industry trends and technologies to incorporate best practices into our projects

You will be responsible for developing and maintaining software applications to support our business objectives. Key responsibilities include writing and testing code, debugging programs, and collaborating with senior developers and other team members to design and implement new features. The role also involves learning and applying best practices in coding, staying updated with the latest programming trends and technologies, and contributing to the overall software development lifecycle
